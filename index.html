About us

We help stray animals to find their home

NGO "Маємо право" (translates from Ukrainian into 'We have the right") - a animals rescue organization based in Lozova, Kharkiv region (Ukraine).

We believe that every cat and dog deserves to have a good life. Together we can bring this wishful thinking into reality.
